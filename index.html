Our Story

Woven on tradition, worn into the present.

Poucha began with a single drape — a grandmother's Kanjivaram, its zari still gleaming after three decades. That saree taught us that a good weave doesn't age, it simply waits for the next shoulder to fall on.

Today we work directly with weaving families across Kanchipuram, Varanasi and Maheshwar, paying fair looms-to-hand prices and carrying their craft — unchanged — into every parcel we send out.

  • Sourced directly from handloom clusters, no middlemen
  • Pure zari and natural dye options on request
  • Every saree paired with drape & care notes
